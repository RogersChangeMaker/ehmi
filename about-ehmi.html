BACKGROUND



Eternal Hope Ministries International was conceived in the place of prayer through a deep undying passion for souls. While the founder (Rev Wase Rogers) was spending some time in prayer, the LORD laid in his heart a passion for souls by asking him a question:

"Are you aware that Asia that has 2/3 of the world's population is the least reached place with the gospel?"


This question from the LORD gave Rev Wase the task to start praying for a clear vision on how to satisfy the LORD'S concern. Then in the year 2020 the LORD gave him the name Eternal Hope Ministries International as a means of fulfilling the mandate. Him and his wife needed an Asian experience, so they sold everything they had and traveled first to Bahrain (Middle East Asia) in February 2022 where they began a work, planted two fellowships of about 25 people. Rev Wase returned to Cameroon in February 2023 while his wife remained in Bahrain to consolidate the work, do some job to support him as he prepared to launch to Philippines that the LORD laid in their hearts. Rev Wase then traveled to Philippines in November 2024 to start the work in all its fulness and his wife later on joined him in May 2025.



Rev Wase Rogers served in Discipling the Nations Ministries (DNM) Cameroon for about eighteen (18) years at different functions and cities. He was ordained as a Minister of the gospel in MAY 2013 by the leadership of DNM.

Over a period of twelve (12) years, Rev Wase did so many trainings and studies, some of which are: Youth Ministers Training, School of Missions, Certificate and a Diploma in Ministry. He studied Environmental Science for a Bachelor's Degree and then graduated with a Masters' Degree in Theology in September 2015 from the Global Focus Bible College USA.
