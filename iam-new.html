We welcome you to Eternal Hope Ministries International. This is your home and we shall endeavor to make it as convenient as possible for you to worship the LORD.

HEBREWS 9:27-28 “And just as it is destined that each person dies only one time and after that comes judgment, so also CHRIST JESUS died only one time as a sacrifice to take away the sins of many people. JESUS will come again but not to deal with our sins again. This time He will bring salvation to all those who are eagerly waiting for Him.”



My dear brother, my dear sister, one day you will die and leave this world. Regardless of how you are living your life today, whether in riches or in poverty you will someday die. Then you will meet your creator: GOD Almighty, to judge you of how you lived on earth.
Where will you spend eternity after judgment? In Heaven with the LORD or in Hell Fire with the Satan?
The only way to be sure of heaven is to believe in JESUS and invite Him into your heart as your LORD and SAVIOR. He died on the cross to take away your sins so that you may live a righteous life; so today is your opportunity to make peace with the LORD.

Romans 10:9-10 "For if you confess with your mouth that JESUS is LORD and believe in your heart that GOD raised Him from the dead, you will be saved. For it is by believing in your heart that you are made right with GOD, and it is by confessing with your mouth that you are saved."

So, make this short prayer of confession: LORD JESUS, I INVITE YOU INTO MY HEART, TO BE MY LORD AND SAVIOR. IN JESUS NAME. AMEN.
